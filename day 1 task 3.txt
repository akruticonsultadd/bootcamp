                           
                                 SUBCONTRACTOR AGREEMENT

1.In this agreement the subcontractor provides services to client related to operations whenever client requests and also agrees to provide assistance on behalf of client.
2.The agreement would be of 12 months from the date of execution n if the party wants they can terminate the agreement by giving 2 weeks written noitce to the party at any time. And if the subcontractor refuses to work or fails in his work then they will terminate and he would to go to the remedies.
3.Whatever services subcontractor needs the can bring on monthly basis and or on hourly rate and the clients would have to approve and the payment would be on net 30 days.
